Upcoming Content

Aventurian Magic I (English Translation)

Finally, the English version of TDE for Foundry gets one of the most important and requested Modules. Chock full of spells, cantrips, professions and skills for your magically inclined characters.

Special thanks to our voluntary translators Lambert, Nellkir and VTTom.

Available in the F-Shop.

The Suncoast (German only)

The so far most extensive DSA module for Foundry combines the complete contents of the regional game aid Suncoast with numerous additions from the other Suncoast books and extensive map material. Everything it takes for a game in the south!

Available in the F-Shop.

The Labyrinth of Intrigues (German only)

The exciting adventure not only offers 9 great maps, but also contains over 40 NPCs and creatures with their own tokens and values. Plunge yourself and your players into the labyrinth of intrigues!

Available in the F-Shop.
