3.0.1 Foundry TDE News (12/20/2021)

Foundry V9 Release

Foundry 9 is finally available. The new features include great performance improvements on maps with many walls and light sources, the introduction of map sets, button assignments and much more. Details on the innovations can be found on the Foundry homepage. Future The Dark Eye 5 modules and patches will only be available through Foundry 9.
