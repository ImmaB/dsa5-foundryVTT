Gods of Aventuria I (English Translation)

The module "Gods of Aventuria" is now available in english.

Many thanks to our voluntariy translation helpers Lambert, Nellkir und VTTom.

Available at the F-Shop.

Das aventurische Nekromanthäum (german)

Nekromanthäum is the first foundry module published in parallel with the book release and contains over 50 creatures and five exciting adventure locations inclduing maps as well as rules for summoning various undead beings.

Available (presumably 1/27/2022) at the F-Shop.

Rabenkrieg 2 - Der Biss der Spinne (German)

The second adventure of the "Rabenkrieg" campaign pulls the heroes deeper into the raging war and they have to decide how posterity will one day remember the it and them. The "Biss der Spinne" can also be played as a single adventure and comes with plenty of additional material.

Available soon at the F-Shop.
