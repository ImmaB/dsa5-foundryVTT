2.1.0 Foundry TDE News (7/10/2021)

VTTom's Foundry Channel

VTTom started two youtube series concerning tutorials and patch notes in german. Ever wondered how the merchant feature works? Missed out on recent changes? Check his videos and get up to date. You can find the channel here.

Niemand's Foundry Channel

Niemand also created a channel containing tutorials on foundry and especially The Dark Eye in foundry in german. He is also the first one to create a let's play for foundry and TDE with the adventure "Das Geheimnis des Drachenritters". If you want to learn more find the channel here and have fun.

New Content

Compendium 1 (German)

Compendium 1 (GERMAN version) is finished and will be available soon. The Compendium contains a lot of new features like hitzones and wound thresholds. Hitzones can be manually enabled via configuration. All creatures in all modules should soon be updated with a matching hitzone. Applications (skill subcategories) will also now be available for skills.

Available in the F-Shop.
